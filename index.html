A simple guide to applying Color wheel & Typography for Zhillsystems on the web by hellodemola

Background story

Working with a brand like Zhillsystems, it is unsurprising to get the offer to improve not just the functionality of her web application but the 'look and feel' as well. The brand loves to push beyond their previous sense of achievement and that is always reflected in the overall aspect of the business including her website.

Brief

After the first design in 2018 by hellodemola,
the focus now is to make web application:

  • Very light weight to increase load time
  • Communication should feel clear, conceit and coherent
  • Use Illustrations and Icons in place of images
  • For inspirtation: client suggestes:
    • Pratica
    • Oropay
